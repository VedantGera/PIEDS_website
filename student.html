Student Cell

The Student Cell of PIEDS provides a strong support to the day to day operations of the incubator. With their creativity, innovation and risk taking ability, they lead important verticals and enable PIEDS in outreach, events, programs, technology and research. We thank them for the energy and commitment they bring with them.


Prathamesh Ajay Baisware

Lead Agri Incubation team

Prathamesh is a third-year student in BITS Pilani. His interests range from finance, entrepreneurship, social service to product management and business analytics. In the Agri Incubation team of PIEDS, he'll work on setting up the Agri and rural incubation program, outreaching various entrepreneurs, and working on activities to encourage startups in the field of Agriculture

Keshav Bansal

Lead Events team

Keshav is a sophomore at BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus. He has a keen interest in product design and startup culture. In the events and conferences vertical of PIEDS, he will be responsible for conducting high quality workshops and competitions to encourage the spirit of entrepreneurship in students.
