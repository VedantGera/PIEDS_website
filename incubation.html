Incubation
  • Business acceleration & Funding Support is an extremely focused 'high growth' phase of this program, designed for the startups with a market ready product. Few selected startups will be fast tracked with 'grant-in-aid' funding support of upto INR 30 Lakhs, which will be made available to startups in tranches or milestones driven approach, and 'on ground' focused hand holding.
  • Startups will validate their products/service/technology.
  • Startups will be mentored on how to commercially launch their product/service and sustain in marketplaces.
  • Marketing & Mentoring support by industry experts will be provided during the program.
